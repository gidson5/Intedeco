Our ethos

To us,design is more than more pretty pictures

its about understanding a brand,the atmosphere it presents and

the environment in which it operates.We have a genuine passion that

creates brand experiences that are unique and individual,but coupled with a realistic and

determined view that design should sell.

OUR BACKGROUND

Modern space is a luxury design agency,our background spans the breadth of design from

branding to websites,prints and product developement and for clients in a range of sectors

including:interior design,achitecture,property developement,domes and retails.

We come from a range of backgrounds of both agency and clients side,in over 5 years,nearly

all of our works have come from clients referrals and from work that we have produced.

this allows us to keep our business quite private and for us to be selective of the projects we undertake.

We are a small,senior team and is focused primarily on the needs of our clients.we believe we

are different but not just for the sake of it.we take your business as serious as we do to our own.

not only because our clients are our business but because we are proud of what we do and want

you to be too.
